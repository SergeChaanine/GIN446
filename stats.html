How to read these numbers

DataReportal (Kepios) aggregates platform and survey data and reports country-level internet user estimates, while the World Bank/ITU provide development indicators often based on national surveys and administrative data. Speedtest (Ookla) measures network performance via active tests and reports median download speeds; these are useful for comparing measured user experience but depend on where and when tests were run. The References page provides direct links to the cited datasets.
